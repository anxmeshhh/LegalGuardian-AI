RESIDENTIAL LEASE AGREEMENT

This Residential Lease Agreement ("Lease") is entered into as of March 1, 2025, by and between Greenfield Properties LLC ("Landlord") and Alex Rivera ("Tenant").

Section 1. Premises
The Landlord agrees to lease to the Tenant the residential property located at 456 Oak Street, Apt 3B, Austin, TX 78701 ("Premises") for use as a private residence only.

Section 2. Term
The initial lease term shall be twelve (12) months, commencing on March 1, 2025, and ending on February 28, 2026. This Lease shall automatically renew for successive twelve-month terms unless the Tenant provides written notice of non-renewal at least ninety (90) days prior to the expiration of the then-current term. The Landlord may elect not to renew by providing thirty (30) days' notice.

Section 3. Rent
The Tenant shall pay monthly rent of $2,200, due on the first day of each month. A late fee of $150 shall be charged for any payment received after the fifth day of the month. The Landlord reserves the right to increase rent by up to 15% upon each renewal, at the Landlord's sole discretion.

Section 4. Security Deposit
The Tenant shall pay a security deposit of $4,400 (equivalent to two months' rent). The Landlord may deduct from the security deposit any amounts for damages beyond normal wear and tear, unpaid rent, cleaning fees, and any other costs incurred due to the Tenant's occupancy, as determined by the Landlord in its sole discretion. The security deposit, less any deductions, shall be returned within sixty (60) days after the Tenant vacates the Premises.

Section 5. Maintenance and Repairs
The Tenant shall be responsible for all maintenance and repairs to the Premises, including but not limited to plumbing, electrical, HVAC systems, appliances, and structural elements. The Tenant shall maintain the Premises in good condition and shall not make any alterations without the Landlord's prior written consent.

Section 6. Liability and Indemnification
The Tenant shall indemnify, defend, and hold harmless the Landlord from any and all claims, damages, injuries, losses, and expenses arising from the Tenant's use of the Premises, including injuries to third parties. The Tenant assumes all risk of loss or damage to personal property. The Landlord shall have no liability for any damage to the Tenant's property or person, regardless of cause, including the Landlord's own negligence.

Section 7. Early Termination
If the Tenant terminates this Lease before the end of any term, the Tenant shall pay a termination fee equal to three (3) months' rent plus forfeit the entire security deposit. The Landlord may terminate this Lease at any time with fourteen (14) days' notice if the Landlord determines, in its sole discretion, that the Tenant has violated any term of this Lease.

Section 8. Entry and Inspection
The Landlord reserves the right to enter the Premises at any time for inspection, maintenance, or showing to prospective tenants or buyers, without prior notice to the Tenant.

Section 9. Insurance
The Tenant shall obtain and maintain renter's insurance with a minimum coverage of $100,000 and shall name the Landlord as an additional insured party.

Section 10. Pets
No pets of any kind are permitted on the Premises. A violation of this provision shall result in an immediate fine of $500 and shall constitute grounds for immediate termination of this Lease.

Section 11. Governing Law and Dispute Resolution
This Lease shall be governed by the laws of the State of Texas. Any disputes shall be resolved through binding arbitration in Travis County, Texas. The Tenant waives any right to a jury trial.

Section 12. Entire Agreement
This Lease constitutes the entire agreement between the parties and supersedes all prior discussions and agreements. This Lease may not be modified except in writing signed by both parties.
